About the Master

Nikita is a professional massage therapist with over 4 years of experience in the field. His deep understanding of body mechanics and recovery stems from his medical background — he earned his medical degree in 2019.

His journey into rehabilitation began back in 2016 when he enrolled in medical college. Since then, he has continued to develop his expertise, eventually gaining a formal qualification as a rehabilitation specialist.

Nikita offers over 15 types of massage techniques, including classic, sports, relaxing, anti-cellulite, and more. This allows him to tailor each session to the unique needs of every client.

In addition, Nikita holds the title of Candidate for Master of Sports in Judo (柔道). His athletic background gives him a deeper perspective on physical recovery and helps him guide clients through effective body rehabilitation.
